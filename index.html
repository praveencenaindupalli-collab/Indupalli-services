About Founder

Praveen Indupalli

Founder & Managing Director

Praveen Indupalli is dedicated to delivering trusted staffing and recruitment solutions that connect talented professionals with the right career opportunities. With a strong vision for helping job seekers and businesses grow together, he founded Indupalli Services to simplify the hiring process and create meaningful employment opportunities.

At Indupalli Services, we focus on professional staffing, recruitment support, candidate sourcing, and workforce solutions across multiple industries. Our goal is to provide reliable hiring services for businesses while helping candidates find rewarding careers through a smooth and professional application process.

Under the leadership of Praveen Indupalli, the company is committed to professionalism, trust, quality hiring, and long-term relationships with both employers and job seekers. We aim to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity through innovative recruitment solutions and dedicated support.
